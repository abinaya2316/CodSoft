About Us

  • Welcome to our heaven of words and flavors, where every sip tells a story and every page invites exploration. and dive into the world of literature with a steaming cup of your favorite brew, surrounded by the comforting embrace of books.
  • Discover the perfect blend of relaxation and inspiration at our cafe cum library, where every visit promises a delightful journey of taste and imagination.
  • Step into a world where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee mingles with the scent of old books, creating a unique atmosphere that sparks creativity and relaxation. Our cafe cum library is a sanctuary for book lovers and coffee aficionados alike, offering a curated collection of literary treasures alongside a menu of delicious beverages and snacks.
  • As you settle into a cozy corner with your favorite book in hand, let the words transport you to distant lands and intriguing stories. Our shelves are filled with classics, bestsellers, and hidden gems waiting to be discovered, creating a haven where every reader can find their next adventure.
  • Sip on a rich espresso or indulge in a decadent pastry as you lose yourself in the pages of a novel or engage in lively discussions with fellow book enthusiasts. Our friendly staff is always ready to recommend the perfect read or craft your ideal coffee concoction, ensuring that each visit is a delightful experience for all your senses.
  • Whether you're seeking a quiet escape, a place to study, or a cozy spot for literary gatherings, our cafe cum library welcomes you with open arms. Come join us and let the magic of books and coffee ignite your imagination and rejuvenate your spirit.

Events

Book Club Meeting

Join our vibrant book club meetings where pages come alive with discussions that ignite imagination, foster friendships, and unveil the magic of storytelling! Dive into captivating plots, explore intriguing characters, and share your literary insights in a welcoming and lively atmosphere. Discover new perspectives, make meaningful connections, and immerse yourself in the joy of reading with us

Author's Talk

Embark on an enlightening journey at our Author's Talk events, where words weave tales of inspiration, creativity, and boundless imagination! Immerse yourself in captivating discussions led by renowned authors, delve into their literary worlds, and gain insights into their writing process, inspirations, and the stories behind the stories. Connect with fellow book enthusiasts, expand your literary horizons, and let the magic of words ignite your passion for storytelling!

Open Mic Night

Step into the spotlight at our Open Mic Nights, where talent takes center stage and creativity knows no bounds! Whether you're a poet, musician, storyteller, or performer of any kind, our stage is yours to share your passion and shine. Join a supportive community, showcase your unique voice, and let your artistry resonate in a warm and welcoming atmosphere. Unleash your creativity, connect with like-minded artists, and make unforgettable memories at our Open Mic Nights!

Literary Film Screening

Experience literature come to life on the big screen at our Literary Film Screening! Sit back, relax, and enjoy cinematic adaptations of beloved books and literary classics. From gripping dramas to whimsical fantasies, our film screenings bring stories to vivid life, sparking discussions and celebrating the art of storytelling in visual form. Grab some popcorn and immerse yourself in a world of cinematic wonders!
